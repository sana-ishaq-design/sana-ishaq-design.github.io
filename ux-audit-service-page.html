05 — The Audit Process

Five steps. Fixed timeline.
No ambiguity.

Every audit follows the same structured process — so you know exactly what happens, when, and what you receive at the end. No process surprises, no scope creep, no report delays.

01
Product Review
Discovery call to understand your business goals, target users, and priority flows. I ask for access to your live product, staging environment, or Figma prototypes — plus any analytics data you have.
02
Usability Evaluation
End-to-end expert review of your product against Nielsen's 10 heuristics and Baymard Institute research. Every screen logged, every friction point annotated, severity rated at Critical / High / Medium.
03
Analysis & Prioritisation
Findings ranked by user impact and conversion cost. Quick wins identified separately from structural issues. Priority matrix built for immediate team use — not just a list of problems, but an ordered action plan.
04
Audit Report Delivery
Full written report with annotated screenshots, severity ratings, prioritised recommendations, and a conversion analysis. Delivered as a structured PDF and Notion/Figma doc formatted for sharing with your development team.
05
Walkthrough Session (60 min)
Live review of every finding with your team — explaining the "why" behind each issue, answering implementation questions, and advising on approach. Recorded and shared with you for async reference.
