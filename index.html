HaierMall Redesign
Recommended: 1200 × 750px
+31%Online Sales

E-commerce · Web

Redesigned HaierMall to increase online sales by 31% through a more trusted shopping experience.

Problem
A mid-to-premium appliance brand's black-and-white, brand-free UI was eroding trust at the exact moment buyers needed confidence — product prices ranged from budget to high-ticket.
Solution
Heuristic audit + Baymard Institute research led to a full design system with brand identity, restructured product hierarchy, and a checkout trust overhaul zero functionality changes, pure UX.
Outcome
+31% online sales,−20% bounce rate, +40% faster product discovery confirmed 3 months post-launch.
